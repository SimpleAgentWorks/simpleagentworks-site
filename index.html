What is OpenClaw?
OpenClaw is an AI agent platform built for real-world business automation. It runs locally on your Mac mini, giving you full control over your agents without relying on cloud subscriptions or third-party APIs eating into your margins. Think of it as the operating system for your AI automation business — it's what your agents live on, and what you'll use to build, deploy, and manage client workflows.
Do I need coding experience to do this?
No. OpenClaw is built for builders, not developers. The workshop is designed to take someone with zero technical background and give them the skills to build and sell real AI automation. That said, if you do have a technical background, you'll be able to go deeper and build more sophisticated solutions. Either way, you're covered.

If you follow the plan and run into roadblocks, reach out — that's exactly what the live support and community are for. Most people who do the work see results in their first month.

The Mac mini is yours to keep. It's not a rental or a lease. You own the hardware outright as part of your purchase.
